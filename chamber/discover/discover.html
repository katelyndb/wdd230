Parkview High School

Parkview High School was built in 1976 in Lilburn Georgia. It is operated by Gwinnett County Public Schools. It is a public school that ranges from grade 9-12. Many events and games are hosted at this school. Go Panthers!

Camp Creek Greenway

4.2-mile multi-use trail originating in Lilburn City Park. In 2014, the greenway was dedicated as a National Wildlife Federation Certified Wildlife Habitat.

Park Guidelines

  • Keep right except to pass.
  • Pedestrians have the right-of-way.
  • Announce yourself as you approach from behind. Example: “On your left”.
  • Bike and skate speeds must be controlled to prevent unsafe conditions.
  • Georgia Bicycle Helmet Law is enforced.
